FAQ
Which image formats are supported?
PDFMASTER supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. These cover the vast majority of image files you will encounter on the web and from cameras.
Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?
Yes! You can select as many images as you like. They will each be placed on their own page in the final PDF. You can also reorder them before converting.
Will the image quality be preserved?
Yes, images are embedded at their original resolution in the PDF. There is no lossy re-compression applied, so your photos stay sharp and detailed.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. All conversion happens locally in your web browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
