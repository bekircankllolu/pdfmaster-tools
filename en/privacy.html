PDFMASTER Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

At PDFMASTER (pdfmaster.tools), we respect your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use our site and how it is used.

1. Data Collection

PDFMASTER runs entirely in your browser. The PDF, image, Word, Excel, or PowerPoint files you upload are never sent to any server. All processing happens on your device and your files are only accessible to you.

2. Cookies

Our site uses cookies and localStorage for the following purposes:

  • Remembering your language preference
  • Storing your theme (dark/light mode) preference
  • Maintaining the processed file counter
  • Collecting anonymous usage statistics via Google Analytics (with your consent)

3. Third-Party Services

Our site may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous visitor statistics
  • Google AdSense: Ad display
  • CDN Services: Loading library files (jsDelivr, cdnjs)

4. Data Security

Your files are never uploaded to our servers. All conversion, merging, and editing operations are performed in your browser using JavaScript. Secure connection is provided via HTTPS encryption.

5. User Rights

You can delete cookies and clear localStorage data at any time through your browser settings. For any questions, please contact us at info@pdfmaster.tools.

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on our site.
