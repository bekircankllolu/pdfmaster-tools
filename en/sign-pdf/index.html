FAQ
Is a digital signature legally binding?
PDFMASTER provides a visual signature overlay on your PDF. For informal agreements and personal documents this is typically sufficient. For legally binding e-signatures with certificates, consult a dedicated e-signature service.
Can I sign on a phone or tablet?
Yes! The signature pad works great with touchscreens. Drawing your signature with a finger or stylus on a tablet gives the most natural result.
Is my signed document stored anywhere?
No. Your PDF and signature are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored on any server. Once you close the page, all data is gone.
Can I add multiple signatures to one document?
Yes, you can place your signature on multiple pages within the same document. Each signature can be positioned and sized independently.
