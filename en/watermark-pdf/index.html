FAQ
Can I add a watermark to all pages at once?
Yes, the watermark is applied uniformly to every page in your PDF document with a single click. No need to add it page by page.
Can I control the watermark transparency?
Absolutely. You can adjust the opacity from nearly invisible to fully opaque. A semi-transparent watermark lets the document content remain readable while still marking the document.
What text can I use as a watermark?
You can type any text you want. Common examples include "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", "SAMPLE", your company name, or a copyright notice. There is no character limit.
Is the watermark permanent?
The watermark is embedded into the PDF as an overlay. While it is visible on every page, your original file remains unchanged — the tool creates a new watermarked copy.
