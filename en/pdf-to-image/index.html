FAQ
What image formats can I export to?
You can export PDF pages as either JPG or PNG images. JPG is ideal for photos and general use with smaller file sizes, while PNG is better for documents with text and graphics that need lossless quality.
What resolution are the exported images?
Images are exported at high resolution suitable for both screen viewing and printing. You can adjust the quality settings to balance between file size and image clarity.
Can I convert specific pages only?
Yes, you can select individual pages to convert instead of processing the entire document. This is useful when you only need a few pages as images.
Is my PDF uploaded to any server?
No, never. The entire conversion process runs locally in your browser. Your PDF file and the generated images stay on your device at all times.
