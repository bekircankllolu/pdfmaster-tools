FAQ
Is the PDF merge tool really free?
Yes, PDFMASTER's merge tool is completely free with no hidden costs, no registration required, and no file limits. You can merge as many PDFs as you need without paying anything.
Are my files secure when merging PDFs?
Absolutely. Your files never leave your browser. PDFMASTER processes everything client-side using JavaScript, so no data is uploaded to any server. Your documents remain 100% private.
How many PDF files can I merge at once?
There is no limit on the number of files. You can merge as many PDFs as you want. The only practical constraint is your device's available memory.
What is the maximum file size for merging?
Each individual PDF file can be up to 100MB. Since processing happens locally in your browser, larger files may take a bit longer depending on your device's performance.
