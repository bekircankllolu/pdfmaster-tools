FAQ
Will compressing my PDF reduce its quality?
The quality loss is minimal, especially at low and medium compression levels. Text and vector graphics remain sharp. Only embedded images are optimized, and you can preview the result before downloading.
How much can I reduce my PDF file size?
Depending on the content and compression level, you can reduce file sizes by up to 70%. PDFs with lots of high-resolution images see the biggest size reductions.
Is the compression process secure?
Yes, completely. Your PDF is processed entirely within your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your files remain private and secure on your device at all times.
What is the maximum file size I can compress?
You can compress PDFs up to 100MB in size. Since all processing is done locally, performance depends on your device's capabilities.
